Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our 24-hour technical SEO fix process.

Why should I pay ₹2,500 for GA4 setup?

Most "free" setups only track pageviews. Our technical setup includes Custom Events, Scroll Tracking, and Lead Form triggers via GTM, giving you the actual ROI data you need to grow your business in India.

How exactly do you setup GA4 for my site?

We don't just paste a code. We configure your Data Stream, exclude internal traffic (so your own visits don't mess up data), enable Enhanced Measurement, and set up custom dimensions. This ensures your data is clean and actionable from Day 1.

Do I get a Dashboard to see my data?

Yes. For our premium setups, we link your GA4 data to a Google Looker Studio Dashboard. This gives you a one-page "Business Health" view of your traffic, conversions, and ROI without you having to navigate the complex GA4 interface.

Why do you use GTM for tracking?

Google Tag Manager (GTM) is the "brain" of your tracking. Instead of hard-coding tags, we use GTM to trigger events like Form Submits, WhatsApp Clicks, and Video Views. It makes your site faster and your tracking 100% accurate without breaking your backend code.

How do you ensure tracking is 100% accurate?

We use the GTM Debug Mode and GA4 DebugView to run live tests on your site. We simulate a customer journey—clicking buttons and filling forms—to verify that every single "Goal" is being recorded correctly before we hand over the project.

Do you work with Shopify, WordPress, or Wix?

Yes. We specialize in technical fixes across all major platforms used by Indian SMBs. Whether it is a custom Cloudflare/HTML site or a Shopify store, we can fix the tracking in 24 hours.

Is this a monthly retainer service?

No. We believe in the Affordable Indian Economy model. You pay a one-time fee per fix. No hidden monthly "Agency Tax." You only pay for the specific technical work we deliver.

How do you fix GSC Indexing issues so fast?

We don't just "request indexing." We audit your Robots.txt, Sitemap.xml, and 404 redirects, then use the IndexNow API and manual GSC pings to force Google to crawl your new content immediately.

I'm not in Jaipur. Can you still help?

Absolutely. While we are based in Jaipur, 90% of our clients are from Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, and across India. We work 100% remotely via secure temporary access.

What if the tracking breaks again?

Every fix comes with 7 days of priority support. For our Backend/CRM clients, we also offer an optional ₹500/month "Peace of Mind" support plan to keep your database synced 24/7.
