Website Development Cost in India (2026): Complete Pricing Guide

Confused about website pricing? This detailed guide explains how much a website really costs in India in 2026 — for small businesses, ecommerce sellers, startups and growing brands.

Website development cost in India varies widely because businesses use websites for very different goals. A local shop, an ecommerce seller, and a startup product cannot be priced the same — even if the sites look similar on the surface.

The real question is not “How many pages?” but “What should this website do for my business?”

Reality check: A ₹5,000 website and a ₹50,000 website may look similar, but differ massively in speed, SEO readiness, scalability and conversion ability.

Website Cost by Type (Ideal Pricing in India)

  • Single-page / landing page: ₹3,000 – ₹8,000
  • Small business website (5–7 pages): ₹8,000 – ₹20,000
  • Professional corporate website: ₹20,000 – ₹50,000
  • Ecommerce website: ₹25,000 – ₹1,20,000+
  • Startup / SaaS website: ₹20,000 – ₹80,000+

These price ranges reflect realistic market pricing in India for websites that are mobile-friendly, fast and SEO-ready — not inflated agency retainers.

Website Cost Breakdown by Business Need

Website Cost for Small Businesses

For small and local businesses, a website’s primary role is trust, visibility and enquiries. Advanced features are rarely needed.

  • 5–7 core pages (Home, About, Services, Contact, Gallery, FAQs)
  • Mobile-first layout for Android users
  • WhatsApp, Call & Google Maps integration
  • Basic on-page SEO for local searches

Ideal cost: ₹8,000 – ₹20,000

A clean, fast website that generates calls or WhatsApp messages is far more valuable than a cheaper site that only “looks okay”.

Ecommerce Website Cost in India

Ecommerce websites cost more because they handle payments, orders and customer trust. Performance and checkout experience directly impact revenue.

  • Product catalogue & categories
  • Payment gateway & secure checkout
  • Order, tax & inventory management
  • Mobile-optimised buying experience

Ideal cost: ₹25,000 – ₹1,20,000+

Very low-cost ecommerce websites often fail due to slow checkout, poor mobile UX, or lack of SEO structure.

Startup Website Cost Breakdown

Startups should prioritise speed and flexibility over heavy design investments. A lean website helps validate ideas faster and saves capital.

  • MVP or landing-page style structure
  • Clear product or service positioning
  • Email capture or demo request flow
  • SEO-ready foundation for future growth

Typical startup budgets:
MVP website: ₹10,000 – ₹20,000
Investor-ready website: ₹20,000 – ₹40,000
Product-led or SaaS website: ₹40,000+

Landing Page vs Full Website Cost

Not every marketing campaign needs a full website. In many cases, a focused landing page converts better and costs less.

Landing page: ₹3,000 – ₹8,000
Best for ads, lead generation, launches and WhatsApp funnels.

Full website: ₹8,000 – ₹50,000+
Best for branding, SEO and long-term growth.

Website Redesign Cost

Website redesign is not just about changing colours. It often involves fixing mobile usability, performance and SEO gaps.

Typical redesign cost: 40% – 70% of the original website cost

Monthly Website Maintenance Cost

Maintenance keeps your website secure, fast and updated. Ignoring it usually leads to higher repair costs later.

  • Small business website: ₹1,000 – ₹2,000 / month
  • Ecommerce website: ₹2,000 – ₹5,000 / month
  • High-traffic or custom websites: ₹5,000+

Hidden Website Costs Most Businesses Miss

  • Domain renewal & hosting charges
  • Content writing or image licensing
  • Security updates & backups
  • SEO improvements after launch

Understanding these early helps businesses avoid unpleasant surprises later.

Frequently Asked Questions About Website Cost

How much does a website cost in India?

Website development cost in India typically ranges from ₹3,000 for a basic landing page to ₹1,20,000 or more for ecommerce and custom websites, depending on features, design quality and business goals.

What is the cost of a small business website?

A small business website in India usually costs between ₹8,000 and ₹20,000 for a mobile-friendly, SEO-ready website with WhatsApp and contact integration.

How much does an ecommerce website cost in India?

Ecommerce websites in India generally cost between ₹25,000 and ₹1,20,000 or more, depending on product count, payment gateway setup, checkout experience and performance requirements.

Is website maintenance required every month?

Monthly website maintenance is recommended for security updates, backups and performance optimisation. Costs typically range from ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 per month depending on website type.
