Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EzyGo Analytics.

How is this different from Google Analytics?

GA4 is built for data scientists. EzyGoDigi is built for business owners. We focus on actionable data—like which WhatsApp button got clicked—without the complex setup or "Big Tech" bloat.

Do I need to show a Cookie Banner?

No. We are 100% cookieless. Since we don't track PII (Personally Identifiable Information), you can legally remove those annoying popups that hurt your conversion rates.

Will it slow down my website?

Our script is under 5KB—that's smaller than a single low-res icon. It loads asynchronously, ensuring your Core Web Vitals and SEO scores stay perfect.

Can I track WhatsApp clicks?

Yes! It happens automatically. Any link leading to WhatsApp, a phone dialer, or Instagram is captured as a "Lead Event" without you writing a single line of code.

Is it compliant with India's DPDP Act?

Yes. We process all data with a privacy-first approach. We don't store IP addresses or sensitive user data, making you fully compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely. You can download your visitor reports and event logs in CSV or PDF format with one click for your weekly team meetings.

What if I have multiple websites?

Our dashboard supports multi-site management. You can track your main portal and your landing pages from a single login with ease.

Do I need a developer for setup?

If you can copy-paste, you can set it up. Just drop our one-line script into your website header. If you're using WordPress or Shopify, it takes less than 60 seconds.
